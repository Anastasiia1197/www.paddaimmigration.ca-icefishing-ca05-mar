Paddai Immigration Cookie Policy – Transparency and Control

At Paddai Immigration, we place enormous importance on your privacy and clarity of communication. This cookie policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience on our immigration advisory site and client portal, while protecting your personal data in accordance with stringent Canadian privacy regulations under PIPEDA (Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act) 🇨🇦.

How cookies work on our immigration advisory portal

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our site. They help us remember your preferences (such as language), optimise our site performance, and ensure a smooth, personalised browsing experience. Without some essential cookies, features such as remembering login status for our client portal or language settings would not work correctly. For individuals exploring Canadian immigration options, these cookies help us deliver a seamless experience tailored to your needs.

What data we collect and why?

We collect only the data necessary to provide you with a secure, stable, and useful experience on our information platform. This includes technical information about your device and browser, anonymous usage statistics (e.g., most-read pages about Express Entry, PNP programs, or study permits), and your preferences. We use this data solely for legitimate, limited purposes, such as improving our content and services for our visitors across Canada. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes without your explicit consent. All data handling complies with Canadian privacy law and the standards expected of regulated immigration consultants.

External service providers and tracking

We work with reputable external entities (such as analytics platforms) that help us understand site traffic and optimise our services. These partners are contractually bound to the same high standards of data protection that we apply. Any tracking by third parties is conducted with respect for your privacy and in full compliance with PIPEDA and applicable Canadian privacy legislation. Our analytics help us understand which immigration resources are most valuable to our clients.

Your control over cookie preferences

You retain full control over your cookie preferences at any time. During your first visit to our site, you can use our cookie banner to choose which types of cookies (aside from strictly necessary ones) you wish to accept. You can later change your preferences at any time using your web browser settings or by checking the privacy settings in your client account (if applicable). As a Canadian immigration advisory service, we respect your right to privacy and provide these controls transparently.

Updates and contact

This cookie policy may be periodically updated to meet new legal requirements or improved practices. Important changes will be communicated appropriately on our site. If you have any questions regarding this cookie policy, please contact us via the designated channels. Our Privacy Officer can be reached at privacy@paddaimmigration.ca for any cookie-related inquiries.

Types of cookies we use

  • Essential cookies: Necessary for the basic functions and security of the site, including client portal access.
  • Preference cookies: Store your language, regional, and display settings for a personalized experience.
  • Statistical/Analytical cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site so we can improve immigration content and resources.
  • Marketing cookies: Used to measure the relevance of potential informational advertisements (opt-in only).
  • Performance cookies: Optimise the loading speed and responsiveness of our pages across Canada.

Your privacy rights and control

  • Accept or reject cookies during your first visit via the cookie banner.
  • Adjust your preferences at any time using your browser settings.
  • Enable or disable specific categories of cookies (e.g., marketing).
  • Submit a request to access or correct your personal data under PIPEDA.
  • Object to automated processing or profiling.
  • Withdraw consent for non-essential cookies at any time.

At Paddai Immigration, we believe that transparency and control are the foundations of trust. This cookie policy is designed to give you full insight and control over your data, while offering the most useful and friendly informational experience about Canadian immigration programs, pathways, and settlement services. By continuing to use our platform, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy.
